Interesting Pigeon Facts

Fact 1

Pigeons are one of the oldest domesticated birds, dating back over 5,000 years.

Fact 2

Pigeons have excellent navigation skills and can find their way home from over 1,000 miles away.

Fact 3

They can fly at speeds of up to 80 miles per hour and cover distances of 600 miles in a single day.
